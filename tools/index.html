Privacy alternatives to an invasive web

At present, companies like Facebook, Google, and Apple consistently invade the privacy of oblivious consumers. Luckily, there are pro-privacy alternatives to many of the services that these companies provide.

Web Browser: Brave

Brave Web Browser is based on Chromium (like Google Chrome) but instead of invading privacy, it actively shields you from invasions.

Download Brave

Search Engine: DuckDuckGo

DuckDuckGo is the partner of Brave, and it provides quality search results without invasion of privacy.

Visit DuckDuckGo

Email Client: ProtonMail

ProtonMail provides privacy-focused encrypted email without independent contractors reading your private messages.

Sign up for ProtonMail

Smartphone: Blackphone 2

The Blackphone 2 runs on Google's Android operating system but has a more secure operating system on top of it, which enhances privacy.

Purchase the Blackphone 2

Smart Speaker: Mycroft AI Mark II

The Mycroft AI Smart Speaker is a privacy-focused speaker that gives the best results, not the ones they were paid to give.

Get started with Mycroft AI

Social Media: None

There is no viable option for social media use without being exposed to trackers and targeted advertising. Privacy, though improved if you're using these services through Brave, is virtually impossible to achieve while using social networks.

Operating System: OpenSUSE

OpenSUSE, a Linux operating system, is one of few operating systems left that's entirely open source, free, and has no advertisements on the desktop.

Download OpenSUSE

Document Editor: ONLYOFFICE

ONLYOFFICE offers an alternative to Microsoft Word and Google Docs without privacy invasions using trackers.

Visit ONLYOFFICE

Mapping: OpenStreetMap

OpenStreetMap is a collaborative effort to create a free map of the world and has the highest privacy standards of the mainstream mapping services.

Visit OpenStreetMap

Video Conferencing: Jitsi Meet

Jitsi is an open-source video conferencing application that is fully encrypted, allowing for more security than mainstream application like Zoom and Microsoft Teams.

Download Jitsi Meet
